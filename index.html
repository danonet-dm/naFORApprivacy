Loan Affordability & Stress Test — Privacy Policy

✓ No data collection

This Privacy Policy describes how the FORA — Loan Affordability iOS application ("FORA", "the app", "we") handles your information. The short version: FORA collects nothing. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

FORA does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal or financial information. All data you enter — income, expenses, loan parameters — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and is never sent anywhere.

2. No Account Required

FORA does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide an email address. There is no user registration of any kind.

3. No Analytics or Tracking

FORA does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or tracking libraries. We do not use cookies, pixels, or any other tracking technology.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

Because we collect no data, we share no data with any third parties — including advertisers, data brokers, or partners.

5. Shared Reports (User-Initiated)

FORA allows you to voluntarily export your analysis as an image or PDF and share it using iOS's standard share sheet (AirDrop, Messages, email, etc.). This sharing is entirely initiated and controlled by you. FORA does not receive or store any copy of what you share.

6. Children's Privacy

FORA is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

7. Data Security

All data entered into FORA is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's built-in security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID). We have no access to your device or your data.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will post the updated version at this URL and update the "Last updated" date below.
